Recipe #1 Pesto Pasta:

  • Time: 15 min
  • Serves: 1-2
Ingredients Quantity
Olive oil 2 tablespoon
Pesto Sauce 2 ½ tablespoons
Package Of Pasta 16 ounces
Parmasean Cheese 2 tablespoons
Salt/Pepper Your choosing

Directions:

  • Step 1: Gather all ingredients
  • Step 2: Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Stir in pasta and return to a boil. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and transfer into a large bowl.
  • Step 3: Move on to making the sauce by stirring in pesto, salt, and pepper until warmed through.
  • Step 4: Add your pesto sauce to the hot pasta; stir in grated cheese and toss well to coat.

      • Source: https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/11887/pesto-pasta/

      Recipe #2 Lemon Loaf:

    • Time: 1 hour 15 min
    • Serves: 8
    Ingredients Quantity
    Butter ½ cup
    White sugar ½ cup
    Eggs 2
    Sour cream ½ cup
    Vanilla Extract 1 teaspoon
    Freshly grated lemon zest 1 teaspoon
    All-purpose flour 1 ½ cups
    Cornstarch ¼ cup
    Salt ½ teaspoon
    Baking soda ½ teaspoon
    Confectioners sugar 2 cups
    Lemon juice 3 tablespoons

    Directions:

  • Step 1: Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease an 8x4-inch loaf pan.
  • Step 2: Beat butter and sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until lighter in color and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Beat in sour cream, vanilla, and lemon zest; beat for 2 minutes. Combine flour, cornstarch, salt, and baking soda in a separate bowl. Add flour mixture to butter mixture, beating until just incorporated. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
  • Step 3: Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 30 to 35 minutes. Cool in the pan for 5 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool.
  • Step 4: To make glaze: Whisk confectioners' sugar and lemon juice together in a medium bowl until smooth. Place cooled cake on a serving plate and pour glaze over entire cake; allow glaze to dry before serving.

    • Source: https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/176636/lemon-loaf-cake/

    Recipe #3 Garlic bread:

    • Time: 35 min
    • Serves: 8
    Ingredients Quantity
    Butter, softened ½ cup
    Olive oil 3 tablespoons
    Minced cloves of garlic 4
    Garlic powder 1 tablespoon
    Chopped fresh parsley 1 tablespoon
    Salt ½ teaspoon
    French bread 1 loaf

    Directions:

  • Step 1: Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  • Step 2: Mix butter, olive oil, minced garlic, garlic powder, parsley, and salt in a bowl until well combined.
  • Step 3: Cut bread loaf into 1-inch slices and spread garlic mixture over each slice. Place each on a baking sheet. Wrap with additional aluminum foil.
  • Step 4: Bake in the preheated oven until bread is crispy and heated through, 25 to 30 minutes.

    • Source: https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/273325/homemade-garlic-bread/
